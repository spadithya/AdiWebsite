## Projects

### Maricopa County Home Price Predictor

A machine-learning model trained on 456,000 real Phoenix-area home sales
(2018-2026). Enter property characteristics — square footage, year built,
neighborhood, pool, garage — and get a predicted sale price with an 80%
confidence interval. Random Forest beats a linear baseline by ~40% on MAE.

**Stack:** Python · pandas · scikit-learn · XGBoost · Streamlit
**Model:** XGBoost (chosen for size; Random Forest variant wins by 1% MAE)
**Code:** [github.com/spadithya/maricopa-housing-predictor](https://github.com/spadithya/maricopa-housing-predictor)

[**Try the predictor →**](https://spadida-maricopa-housing-predictor.streamlit.app)

### Maricopa County Housing Stats

An interactive dashboard exploring the same 456,000 sales: price trends
by year, neighborhood comparisons across 147 geographic clusters,
property-class breakdowns, and a sqft-vs-price scatter you can filter.
Built as the EDA companion to the predictor — same data, different lens.

**Stack:** Python · pandas · Plotly · Streamlit
**Code:** [github.com/spadithya/maricopa-housing-stats](https://github.com/spadithya/maricopa-housing-stats)

[**Explore the dashboard →**](https://spadida-maricopa-housing-stats.streamlit.app)
