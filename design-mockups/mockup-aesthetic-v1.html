About

Building mission-critical biomedical sensors,
from photodiode to clinical insight.

I design hardware-algorithm co-designed CMOS imaging sensors for high-sensitivity biomarker detection — the kind of instruments that need to be right the first time, every time.

PhD candidate at Arizona State University working with Dr. Erica Forzani at the Biodesign Institute, in collaboration with Mayo Clinic and Sequitur Health Corporation. Previously co-founded Symbionic, building EEG-driven bionic prostheses in Chennai. I care about the meeting point of careful engineering and human consequence.
